Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in the package price? ▼
Every Travencia package covers return flights, hotel accommodation for your full stay, and private airport transfers — all in one price. Optional activities are add-ons you select during booking, priced per person. Your total is always calculated based on your departure country, so there are no hidden flight surcharges.
How does payment verification work? ▼
Once you submit your booking, our team receives the details instantly via WhatsApp. We review your payment information and send a personal confirmation — usually within 2 hours. For PayPal bookings, we provide our payment address and reference code directly.
Can I make multiple reservations? ▼
Absolutely. After each booking you can immediately start another. All your pending reservations are visible in your account under "My Bookings" — each with its own status, code, and confirmation timeline.
Why does the price differ by country? ▼
Flight costs are the biggest variable in any travel package, and they differ enormously by origin. A round-trip from Senegal to Tokyo costs nearly twice as much as one from Paris. Our pricing engine knows your departure country and adjusts the flight component automatically — so every quote is honest and accurate.
Can I cancel or modify my reservation? ▼
Yes — contact us on WhatsApp with your booking code. Modifications are straightforward before admin confirms. Once confirmed, cancellation terms depend on the airline and hotel selected; our team will walk you through every option and any applicable fees.
Do you offer group travel discounts? ▼
Yes — parties of 6 or more unlock negotiated group rates on flights, hotels, and transfers. Reach out via WhatsApp or the form below and we'll build a custom quote tailored to your group size and destination.
